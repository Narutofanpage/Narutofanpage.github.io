Season 1:

Naruto is a loud, goofy kid who wants to become the best ninja ever. He joins a team with Sasuke and Sakura, and they start going on missions.

Season 2:

They enter a big ninja test called the Chunin Exams. A creepy bad guy named Orochimaru shows up and messes everything up.

Season 3:

The village gets attacked during the exams. Naruto and the other ninjas have to fight to protect their home.

Season 4:

Naruto goes on a mission to find a strong healer named Tsunade to become the next Hokage (village leader). Along the way, he keeps training to get better.

Season 5:

Sasuke decides to leave the village to get stronger. Naruto and his friends try to stop him, but it ends in a big, sad fight.
